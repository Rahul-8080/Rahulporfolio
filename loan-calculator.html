  • 💱 Multi-currency support & conversion

EMI Formula

EMI = [ P × R × (1 + R)N ] / [ (1 + R)N − 1 ]
P = Principal Loan Amount R = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual % ÷ 12 ÷ 100) N = Loan Term in Months
